Kia ora koutou, I'm Isabella, a final year PhD candidate in Finance at the University of Otago, Dunedin, New Zealand -- supervised by Professor Jin E. Zhang and Dr Xinfeng Ruan. I expect to submit my thesis for examination in June 2023.

My research and teaching interest are primarily in the area of Derivatives Market, Quantitative Finance, and Investment. I'm a member of the Derivatives and Quantitative Finance Group (DQFG) and have assisted in teaching BSNS114: Financial Decision Making, FINC306: Derivatives, and FINC405: Mathematical Finance. I have also assisted in teaching FINC102: Business Mathematics, FINC204: Personal Finance, and FINC403: Studies in Capital Markets.

Before starting my PhD, I did a BCom and MFinc at Otago.

Kia ora koutou, I'm Isabella, a Teaching Fellow from department of accountancy and finance at the University of Otago, Dunedin, New Zealand.

My research and teaching interests are primarily in the areas of Derivatives Market, Quantitative Finance, and Investment. I'm a member of the Derivatives and Quantitative Finance Group (DQFG) and have assisted in teaching BSNS114: Financial Decision Making, FINC306: Derivatives, and FINC405: Mathematical Finance. I have also assisted in teaching FINC102: Business Mathematics, FINC204: Personal Finance, and FINC403: Studies in Capital Markets.

Before starting my PhD, I did a BCom and MFinc at Otago.
